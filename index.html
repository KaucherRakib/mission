Mission Statement

Home > Mission Statement

The Mission Of The Housing Opportunities Commission Of Montgomery County Is To Provide Affordable Housing And Supportive Services That Enhance The Lives Of Low- And Moderate-Income Families And Individuals Throughout Montgomery County, Maryland So that:

  • No One In Montgomery County Is Living In Substandard Housing.

  • We Strengthen Families And Communities As Good Neighbors.

  • We Establish An Efficient And Productive Environment That Fosters Trust, Open Communication And Mutual Respect.

  • We Work With Advocates And Providers To Maintain Support For All The Work Of The Commission. To Achieve This Mission, HOC Operates As An Affordable Housing Agency, A Housing Finance Agency And A Housing Developer.
